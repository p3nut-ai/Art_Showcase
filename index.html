Scribbling Soul

What image is formed by your scribble?
- A whale.


How is this image related to you?
- Whales need to consume more in order for them to survive. For example, they need to eat more to sustain their bodies or their needs. And I find it similar to my current mindset because I want to dream BIG and work harder. More, and I don't want to be content with a small dream or life. I want to keep on pushing more and more in order to have my own dream life.


How did you express your creativity in this activity?
-I expressed my creativity through maximizing my materials or by using just a pencil and 1 color.

How did you express your imagination in this activity?
-I expressed my imagination through I expressed my creativity through pointing out the shape of the whale in the scribble.
